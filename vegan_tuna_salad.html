Vegan Tuna Recipe

Description

This ‘Chickpea of the Sea’ Vegan Tuna Salad Sandwich is easy, healthy, delicious and sustainable!

Ingredients

  • 1 Can (14oz) of chickpeas
  • Juice of 1/2 a lemon
  • 1/3 cup vegan mayo
  • 1/4 cup celery
  • 1/4 red onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mash chickpeas with fork.
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients into a large bowl.
  3. Mix all the ingredients together.
  4. Serve on a slice of bread or on a lettuce wrap.
