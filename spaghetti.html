Spaghetti Recipe

Description

Spaghetti is a simple and filling dish that anyone can make!

Ingredients

  • A jar of your preferred tomato-based pasta sauce.
  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Parmesan cheese
  • olive oil/li>

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water until the water is on a rolling boil. Then add in pasta, cooking as is directed on the packaging.
  2. While water is heating up to a boil, heat up pasta sauce on the side in a smaller sauce pan.
  3. Strain pasta once finished and add olive oil into the pasta to prevent sticking. Stir pasta in strainer.
  4. Serve pasta in a dish with the tomato sauce poured over. Add desired amount of parmesan cheese.
